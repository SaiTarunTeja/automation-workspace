To install TestNG in Eclipse, the recommended and most straightforward method is using the Eclipse Marketplace: 

• Launch Eclipse IDE: Open your Eclipse workspace. 
• Open Eclipse Marketplace: Navigate to Help &gt; Eclipse Marketplace.... 
• Search for TestNG: In the "Find" search box, type "TestNG" and press Enter or click the "Go" button. [1, 2]  
• Install TestNG for Eclipse: Locate "TestNG for Eclipse" in the search results and click the "Install" button next to it. [3]  
• Confirm Installation: Eclipse will resolve features. Ensure the TestNG checkbox is selected and click "Confirm." 
• Accept License Agreement: Review the license agreement and click "Finish." 
• Restart Eclipse: Eclipse will begin the installation and may prompt you to restart the IDE to apply the changes. Click "Restart Now." 

After Eclipse restarts, TestNG will be installed and ready for use in your projects. You can verify the installation by right-clicking on a project and checking for the "TestNG" option in the context menu.
